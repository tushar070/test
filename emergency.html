Immediate Help and Crisis Intervention

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

Call 1-800-273-TALK (8255) or text HELLO to 741741. Available 24/7.

Crisis Text Line

Text HELLO to 741741 to connect with a trained crisis counselor. Available 24/7.

Emergency Services

If you or someone else is in immediate danger, call 112 for emergency assistance.

Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA)

Call 91-9820466726 for confidential and free information and treatment referral services.

About Us

We are dedicated to providing immediate support and resources for mental health crises. Our goal is to connect individuals with the help they need as quickly as possible.
