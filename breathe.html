Breathing Exercises for Mental Health

Simple exercises to help you relax and focus

Box Breathing

Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds, exhale for 4 seconds, and hold again for 4 seconds. Repeat this cycle 3-5 times.

4-7-8 Breathing

Inhale through your nose for 4 seconds, hold your breath for 7 seconds, and exhale through your mouth for 8 seconds. Repeat this cycle 3-4 times.

Diaphragmatic Breathing

Place one hand on your chest and the other on your belly. Breathe in deeply through your nose, ensuring that your belly rises more than your chest. Exhale slowly through your mouth. Repeat for 5 minutes.
