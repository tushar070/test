Journaling Prompts for Mental Health

Use these prompts to reflect on your thoughts and feelings.

Prompt 1

What are three things you are grateful for today, and why?

Prompt 2

Describe a recent challenge you faced and how you handled it. What did you learn from the experience?

Prompt 3

What is one thing you can do today to take care of yourself? How will it help your mental well-being?

Prompt 4

How have you been feeling lately? Identify any patterns or triggers and reflect on how they affect your mood.

Prompt 5

What are some positive affirmations you can tell yourself today? Write them down and reflect on their impact.
